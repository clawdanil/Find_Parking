Orbi Near

Orbi Near is built to redefine how you experience the world around you.

We believe discovery should be effortless, intelligent, and beautifully simple. Whether it's finding the perfect café, a hidden bar, a gym, or even parking. Orbi Near brings everything "near you" into one seamless, AI-powered experience.

Designed with a focus on minimalism and precision, Orbi Near blends location intelligence with modern design to help you navigate your surroundings with clarity and confidence.

This is not just another discovery app. It's your local universe, intelligently curated.

We are a small, focused team of two tech-driven builders with a shared passion for creating impactful, AI-powered products. Both of us come from strong engineering backgrounds and have actively contributed to open-source AI applications, shaping systems that prioritize scalability, intelligence, and real-world usability.
